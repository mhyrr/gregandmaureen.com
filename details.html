The Wedding

The wedding will be at St. Bernadette's parish in Silver Spring, Maryland.

From 495 take Rt 193 (University Blvd) North a quarter mile and turn right into the church.

The Reception

The reception will be at the Bethesda Hyatt.

From the wedding, take 495 West to Rt 355 (Rockville Pile/Wisconsin Ave) South. The Hyatt is about 2 miles on the right.

Travel Info

The Hyatt is conveniently located next to the Bethesda Red Line Metro stop. This provides easy access to downtown DC. The best stop for all the museums is Smithsonian. Take the Red Line to Metro Center, then switch to the Orange Line.
